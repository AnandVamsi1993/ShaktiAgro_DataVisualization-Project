PRODUCT ANALYSIS

MS SECTION

Insights

    MS SECTION has the maximum sales in April followed by November, June and May. December is a low season for this product. April also yields the highest profits for MS SECTION.

    Although Feb yields less revenue, the no. of transactions in this month are the second highest overall. Customers do not order bulk amounts in Feb despite the unite price being relatively lesser than highest value(in April).

    Although November has the second highest sales, Freight charges are not proportional to sales value. This shows customers are more willing to carry products themselves. (One reason might be that freight charges in winters are significantly higher). Customers seem to take good advantage of the low unit price of MS SECTION in November. The no. of units ordered in this month are second highest overall.

    Although November is the second highest revenue generating month for MS SECTION, the margins generated are the secind lowest overall. This is due to high TFC and TVC charges.

    Because the unit price is so low in Nov, Shakti Agro can come up with pre-orders ( with delivery of products in jan, Feb or April). A 6 month delivery plan ( i.e delivery in April) contracts can be encouraged..This might increase sales in Nov and Dec.

    To maximize revenues from point 2 and 3, Shakti Agro can come up with attractive freight packages to boost sales.

MS BAR

Insights

    MS BAR generates highest margins in Q1 and Q4. Month wise, April and January were the most profitable months. This is likely due to a higher selling price of the product (40K INR per MT)

    Q2 and Q3 have recorded losses from MS BAR purchases.

    However, April and Jan are the not the highest grossing months for this product. August and Feb have recorded the highest sales and transaction numbers. This is understandably due to a lower Selling/Market price of the product

    Unlike MS SECTION, in the case of MS Bar, Freight charges are the highest in November.

MS FLATS

Insights

    Similar to MS BAR, MS Flats rcorded profitable business in Q1 and Q4. April and May have been the most profitable months. Ooctober recorded the highest losses.

    In terms of revenue, May has the least total sales. This is likely due to a higher market price(41K). August and Feb have recorded the highest revenue and transaction numbers. Margins in these months are very low

    Freight Outward charges are the highest in August and November.

WIRE

    WIRE had 0 sales in Q1 followed by a linear increase in Q2 and Q4.

    Unlike MS BAR and other products, Wire does not show negative margins. Q4 is the most profitablke quarter. Feb is the most profitable month. Sales is August typically do not show great profit. This is primarily due to lower Selling price.

    Freight charges are proportional to the revenue being generated for Wire
